About ACE

Project ACE is a creative agency based in Quezon City. ACE stands for activations, creatives, and events--yes, just those three. A jack-of-all-trades approach to creativity is simply not our thing. We believed that perfection is achieved by devoting one's resources on a few essentials.

So, if you need somebody to pull off the perfect marketing event or activation, you know who to call.
